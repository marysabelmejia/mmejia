Hello there! My name is Marysabel Mejia. I'm originally from Boston, MA. I have also had the great pleasure of living in Washington, DC and currently call South Florida, home.


I obtained a bachelor's degree from Boston University in Speech, Language, and Hearing Sciences with a minor in Deaf Studies. I continued to earn a master's in Speech-Language Pathology. I have passionately served as an SLP, having had the honor of supporting many patients/students to achieve their goals. Given a desire to one day run a clinic, I gained additional skills and knowledge through obtaining an MBA, specializing in Health Service Administration.


Ever hungry to learn a new skill, I enrolled in MIT's xPRO coding course. It has proven to be such a rewarding and challenging experience. Through this site, I hope to share some insights into my skillset and transition from SLP to Techie, as well as some of my background and experiences.
