PacMan
This activity challenged us to take the div style for a simple, black circle and build upon it to make this complex shape.
Building even further upon one-dimensional motion/animation, we were challenged to make this two-dimensional movement happen, while using edge detection. As an added bonus, we could change the animation based on speed, color, direction, and size.
In this exercise, we began to explore controlling the DOM, as we made a single PacMan move back and forth on the screen.

Real Time Bus
This activity involved using array methods previously learned and manipulating the DOM. With the click of a button, a new PacMan is created and at the start of the game, each PacMan moves around the screen at its own pace.
Not only did we manipulate styles in this task, but we got to see what happens when HTML, CSS, and JS come together. The coolest part: the eyes follow your mouse around!
